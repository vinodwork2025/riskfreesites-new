The Problem

Instagram is not a website.

Most interior designers put their best work on Instagram. The photos are great. But when a potential client searches "interior designer in [your city]" on Google, Instagram does not show up. Your website does.

Without a real website, you are invisible to everyone who searches online. And most new clients start with a search. They look at a few websites, compare the work, and reach out to the ones that look professional. If you are not there, they pick someone else.

A website also gives you something Instagram can not. It tells your full story, shows your process, lists your services, and gives visitors a simple way to reach you. All in one place, under your name, not under Instagram's rules.

Questions

Things designers ask us

Can I use my own project photos?
Yes. Your own photos make the site stronger. Send us whatever you have and we will use them. If you do not have professional photos yet, we can use high-quality placeholder images while you build your library.
How is this different from Squarespace or Wix?
With Squarespace or Wix, you pick a template and build it yourself. That takes time and skill. With us, you fill in one form and we build the website for you. You see the finished result before you decide anything.
Do I need a domain name already?
No. You can see your website on a preview link first. If you want to go live, we help you set up a domain. If you already have one, we connect to it.
Will I be able to update the website myself?
Yes. Once you approve the site, we set it up so you can make basic updates yourself. Adding new project photos, updating your services, changing contact details. No coding needed.
