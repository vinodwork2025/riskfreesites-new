Questions

Things people ask us

Do I have to pay anything to get started?
No. We build your website before you pay anything. No credit card, no deposit. You only pay after you see the site and decide you want it.
How long does it take to get my website?
We send your website within 48 hours of your request. You fill out the form, we get to work, and you get a link to your real site.
What if I don't like the website?
You walk away. No charge, no long calls, no hard feelings. The whole point is that you get to see it first before you commit.
What types of businesses do you work with?
We work with all kinds of small and local businesses. Restaurants, clinics, law firms, salons, gyms, real estate agents, contractors, freelancers, and more.
Will my website look like a template?
No. Every website is built around your business, your services, and your brand. It is not a theme or a page builder output.
What happens after I approve the website?
We talk about going live. You get your domain set up, we handle the launch, and your website is live for real customers to find you. Simple.
