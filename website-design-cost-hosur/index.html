What affects website design pricing in Hosur?

Three factors drive most of the cost: number of pages (more pages = more time = higher price), features needed (contact forms are cheap; booking systems and ecommerce add significant cost), and design quality (a template costs less, a custom design costs more). SEO setup and mobile optimisation also affect pricing, though these should be standard in any professional website.

Number of pages

More pages = more time

A 1-page site takes a fraction of the time of a 10-page site. Each page needs content, design, and SEO — expect roughly ₹5,000–₹15,000 per additional page from most designers.

Features & functionality

Simple forms to complex systems

A contact form adds minimal cost. An online booking system, payment gateway, or product catalogue can double or triple the price. Be clear on what you actually need.

Design quality

Template vs. custom design

Template-based websites are cheaper but look generic and often rank poorly. A custom-designed site built for your specific audience and business costs more — but converts significantly better.

SEO & mobile setup

Often sold as an add-on

Many designers in Hosur charge extra for mobile optimisation and local SEO. These should be included by default — always confirm they are part of the quoted price, not billed separately.
