II. On the Practice

Why patients choose Dr. Whitfield.

A quiet Harley Street practice. No billboards. No celebrity endorsements. Just an unhurried consulting room, a single principal surgeon, and a philosophy built around restraint — results that should refine, never announce themselves.

i.

A philosophy of natural results

Surgery should refine, never announce itself. Dr. Whitfield's work is grounded in restraint — results that look natural, never operated on. Patients leave the clinic looking refreshed, not different.

ii.

Considered, unhurried consultation

A full consultation always comes before any procedure is discussed. Surgery is a significant decision, and every patient deserves time to understand their options, their anatomy, and what is realistic for them.

iii.

Complete discretion, always

Many patients are professionals and public figures who value privacy above everything else. Every aspect of the clinic — from consultation to recovery — is designed around that expectation.

My job is to listen first. The procedure comes second. The finest outcomes I have delivered all began the same way — with a patient who felt heard.

Dr. James Whitfield · FRCS (Plast)
III. Specialist Procedures

A focused practice. Surgery done carefully.

Six procedures performed regularly and exceptionally well — rather than a long menu of everything. Every plan is tailored to the patient, never the other way round.

i.
Rhinoplasty
Open and closed rhinoplasty tailored to the face — refining proportion, correcting function, preserving character.
ii.
Facelift
Deep-plane techniques that reposition rather than pull. A natural, rested appearance that ages gracefully.
iii.
Blepharoplasty
Upper and lower eyelid surgery to restore a brighter, more rested expression — without altering character.
iv.
Liposuction
Precision body contouring for stubborn, exercise-resistant areas — planned for a balanced, natural silhouette.
v.
Breast Augmentation
Consultative approach with 3D imaging and implant sizing — proportionate, appropriate, entirely yours.
vi.
Skin Resurfacing
Medical-grade laser and peel programmes — delivered and supervised within the clinic.
