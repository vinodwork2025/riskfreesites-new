Questions? We've Got Answers.

Do you accept my insurance?
Yes — we accept most major plans including Delta Dental, Aetna, Cigna, MetLife, and BlueCross BlueShield. We also offer 0% interest payment plans for any remaining balance. Contact us to verify your specific plan →
How do I book a same-day emergency appointment?
Call us at +1 (512) 489-0012 and we'll do our best to see you the same day. We keep dedicated emergency slots open every morning. You can also use the booking form above.
What happens at my first visit?
Your first visit is relaxed and informative — no surprises. We start with digital X-rays (free for new patients this month), a full exam with Dr. Chen, and a clear conversation about what we found and what we recommend. No pressure, no upsells. Book your first visit today →
Do you treat nervous or anxious patients?
Absolutely. Dental anxiety is very common and Dr. Chen's gentle approach is what patients mention most in their reviews. We explain everything before we do it and offer options like nitrous oxide if needed. Let us know when you book and we'll take extra care →
How much does teeth whitening cost?
In-office whitening starts from $299 and typically delivers 6–8 shades of improvement in a single session. Take-home trays start from $199. Both are far more effective than over-the-counter products. Book a free consultation to find out which is right for you →
Where are you located and is there parking?
We're at 142 Congress Ave, Austin, TX 78701 — in the heart of downtown. Metered street parking is available on Congress Ave, and several garages are within a 2-minute walk. We validate parking for patients. Get directions on Google Maps →
